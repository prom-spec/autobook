OpenLoud turns your ebook library into audiobooks using text-to-speech.

Import EPUB, PDF, or MOBI files and listen to them with high-quality voices. Perfect for commutes, workouts, or when your eyes need a break.

Features:
• Import EPUB, PDF, and MOBI ebooks
• Android system TTS or Microsoft Edge TTS voices
• Background playback with media controls
• Android Auto support for in-car listening
• Adjustable playback speed
• Chapter navigation
• Sentence-level progress tracking
• Clean, modern interface
• 100% free and open source (GPL-3.0)

Edge TTS provides natural-sounding voices but requires an internet connection. The built-in Android TTS works fully offline.

OpenLoud is free, open source, and respects your privacy. No ads, no tracking, no accounts.
